Privacy Policy

Welcome to JokerLudo (ludogrouplink.com). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we may collect and how we use, store, and protect it.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect basic personal information like name and email address when you submit a group or contact us. We also use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as pages visited and time spent on site.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

3. Third-Party Tools

We use third-party services like Google Analytics and WhatsApp links. These platforms may collect data as per their privacy policies. We do not sell your data to any third party.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to improve website functionality and analyze traffic. You can disable cookies via your browser settings.

5. Contact

If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy, please contact us via the contact page.

6. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Please check this page regularly to stay informed.
