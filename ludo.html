How to Play Ludo

  • Each player chooses a color and places their 4 tokens in their home yard.
  • Players take turns rolling the dice.
  • On a player's turn, they roll the dice and must move one of their tokens forward the number of spaces shown on the dice.
  • If a player rolls a 6, they get an extra turn.
  • If a token lands on a space occupied by an opponent's token, the opponent's token is sent back to their home yard.
  • The first player to get all their tokens to the center wins!
